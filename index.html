Abolfazl Hashemi

PhD Candidate

ECE Department, UT Austin

I am a PhD student at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at University of Texas at Austin under supervision of Prof. Haris Vikalo. I received my MSE in Electrical and Computer Engineering department at UT Austin in 2016, and a BS degree in Electrical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology in 2014. I was also a visiting undergraduate researcher at the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology under supervision of Prof. Daniel Palomar in 2013.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in convex and nonconvex Optimization and its application in machine learning, signal processing, and control (see the Research page for more details).

#* Convex and Nonconvex Optimization #* Distributed Optimization #* Discrete Optimization #* Large-Scale Machine Learning #* Representation Learning
